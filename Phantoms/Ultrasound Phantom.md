
**Phase 1**

1. Add Water: 400g.
2. Add Glycerol: 133g.
3. Heat to 100 while mixing.


**Phase 2**

1. Gelatin 57.5g
2. Add Flour: half a teaspoon max.
3. Mix while cooling to 60

**Phase 3**

1. When at 60, hold at 60 and mix for 1 hour.

**Casting**

1. Pour in mold.
2. Place in fridge until set.
